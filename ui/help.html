⚙️ How Sentinel-Flow Works

Sentinel-Flow manages employee contracts using Smart Workflow Automation. Each employee evaluation follows a dynamic rule system, ensuring that onboarding, probation, and final reviews are routed to the correct personnel at the exact right time.

🧠 AI Bias Detection

To ensure fair and equitable evaluations across the workforce, the system integrates advanced linguistic analysis. It actively scans reviewer comments and flags subjective, emotional, or potentially biased language before final HR decisions are formalized.

🔒 Data Security & Governance Vault

Protecting your workforce data is our top priority. All employee records, performance metrics, and smart contracts are heavily encrypted both in transit and at rest.

Approved workflows and final evaluations are stored directly in the Governance Vault. This creates an immutable, tamper-proof audit trail that ensures your organization maintains strict compliance with internal policies and global data privacy regulations.

⚡ Automation & Compliance

Once a workflow reaches its conclusion, decision letters are automatically generated, populated with the correct employee data, and signed digitally, removing manual paperwork bottlenecks.
